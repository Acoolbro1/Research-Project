How much sleep should you be getting

Age Amount of sleep
0-3 months 14-17 hours
4-12 months 12-16 hours
1-2 years 11-14 hours
3-5 years 10-13 hours
6-12 years 9-12 hours

Lots of people dont get the right amount of sleep per day, whether this is to little or too much. Sleep can end up feeling like a chore and can be easy to skip out on, however its incredibly important to not let yourself fall behind on these sort of things. Sleep is an incredibly important part of our life and you cant force your body to just go without it.

Blue Lights

Something else that has a large effect on sleep is use of technology. lots of people are on their phones before they go to bed, however this can cause a rise in difficult to fall asleep and to get good sleep. Phones and other electronics produce blue light, which has the same effect as the sun. so by looking at a screen your brain still thinks that its day time in a way, so you'll end up being more alert, you'l have more energy at night, and your are more likely to feel drowsy during the day.

Things like this can really dangerous. when you brain starts to stuggle to tell when is day time and when is night time, you might find yourself with a huge burst of energy in the middle of the night, you may also find yourself struggling to stay awake while doing something like driving.
